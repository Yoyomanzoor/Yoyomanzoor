- Luminal A = morphology thing
- Can do hormone stuff for luminal A
- Lumpectomy + radiation = mastectomy, + public opinion
- Biggest benefit is radation, then hormone therapy, then chemo
- SLNB for staging before axillary dissection
- Mastectomy if multiple failed lumpectomy or too many nodules
- Can also try immunotherapy
- Goal of radiation: stop local recurrence. Goal of chemo: stop distant recurrence
